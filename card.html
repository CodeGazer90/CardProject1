Penguin

Penguins "fly" through the water fast enough to chase down schools of fish to eat.

  • Scientific Name: Spheniscus demersus
  • Average Length: 18 in.
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Coastal and offshore islands

African penguins are found along coastal areas and offshore islands. Their preferred habitats are flat sandy areas with sparse or abundant vegetation growth, or steep, rocky sites with sparse vegetation. African penguins may forage on their own or in small groups.
